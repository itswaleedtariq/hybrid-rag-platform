# Employee Handbook

## Working Hours
The standard work week is Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Employees receive a one-hour lunch break.

## Annual Leave
Permanent employees receive 20 paid annual leave days per calendar year. Leave requests must be submitted at least five working days before the planned absence. No more than five unused days may be carried into the next calendar year, and carried leave must be used by March 31.

## Sick Leave
Employees receive 10 paid sick days each calendar year. A medical certificate is required for an absence of three or more consecutive working days.

## Remote Work
Employees may work remotely up to two days per week after completing the three-month probation period. Written manager approval is required.

## Information Security
Multi-factor authentication is required on company accounts. Suspected security incidents must be reported within one hour.
